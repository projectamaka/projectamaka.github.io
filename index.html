About Project



We are the PBLEastAfrica Uganda project team from Aalto University for 2020! This year, our team is partnering with a student team from Makerere University to support their modular housing project for the Katanga slum in Kampala. We are extremely excited and grateful for this opportunity to connect with the Makerere students, learn about the community, and see Uganda. We hope you'll join us on our journey by following along! Explore this website to learn more about our team, our partner team, Katanga, and our project.
